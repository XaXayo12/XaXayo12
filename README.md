## About me

I am a self-taught developer focused on automation. I build Minecraft second-account bots and AFK systems that play on their own, along with the tooling and web apps around them.

- **Bots and AFK systems:** second accounts that survive, fight, farm, follow, and keep playing while I am away.
- **Automation and tooling:** turning repetitive, boring tasks into code that just runs.
- **Stack:** Rust for the heavy lifting, TypeScript, Next.js and Node for the web side, Java for Fabric mods.
- **Approach:** self-taught, shipping real projects, learning by building.

## What I am building

- **AFK Companion:** a Minecraft 1.21.11 second-account bot (Rust and Azalea) controlled from a Fabric client mod, with multi-account isolation and an in-game GUI.
- Smarter pathfinding, inventory handling, and coordination across several bots at once.
- Web dashboards and tools to drive and monitor the bots.
